Vibgyor International WLL Established in 2009 and started its packaging and disposable Product Division which has created its own space in Qatar for marketing and distribution of best quality plastic and paper products under the brands V-Pack And Vibgyor Internatonal.

There are varies factors that have contributed towards the tremendous growth of the company. Some of them are the on time delivery of products, use of optimum quality material in the fabrication of products, transparent dealings with the reputed clients, maintenance of quality at all levels, adamant efforts of the team members, etc. We make all these attempts in order to satisfy the clients to the fullest. Additionally, we have garnered a huge clientele for the company’s products.

The quality of products at reliable price with exceptional services has created the brand image in the market within short span of time. Being a Qatari company Vibgyor has market presence in most of the Hyper Market and Super Market catering companies, Hotels, Hospitals and Govt.Departments,in the state of Qatar whichhas also helped us to grow exponentially. Vibgyor is being managed by a team of dedicated qualified and experienced professionals who has adopted policies aimed at developing and enriching the potential of each of its employees.

Our Research and Development team are committed to offer the solution for the package industry by providing innovative packaging solution by regular interaction with the end users. We collaborate with our customer manufactures raw material supplier to ensure a supply chain which transform in to reliable and timely delivery.
